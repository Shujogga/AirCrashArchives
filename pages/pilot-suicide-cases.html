Pilot Suicide Cases in Aviation History

Published July 23, 2025
1982
JAL Flight 350 – First officer deployed thrust reversers mid-air; crashed into Tokyo Bay, 24 killed.
1994
Royal Air Maroc 630 – Pilot nosedived after takeoff; investigators cited intentional act, 44 fatalities.
1997
SilkAir 185 – Nosedive into Musi River; NTSB ruled pilot suicide, local inquiry inconclusive (104 killed).
1999
EgyptAir 990 – Relief pilot disengaged autopilot and cut engines; NTSB ruled suicide (217 fatalities).
1999
Air Botswana – Pilot commandeered aircraft and crashed into parked jets; ruled suicide.
2013
LAM Mozambique TM470 – Captain altered autopilot and flew into swamp; suspected suicide (33 killed).
2014
MH370 – Disappeared en route to Beijing; widely suspected pilot suicide (239 presumed).
2015
Germanwings 4U9525 – Co-pilot locked captain out and flew into the Alps; confirmed suicide (150).
2022
MU5735 – Near-vertical dive; flight data suggests deliberate input (132 killed).
2025
AI171 – Fuel selectors cut after takeoff; investigation ongoing, possible pilot suicide (279).
