Air France Flight 447 – CVR Transcript

Date: June 1, 2009

Aircraft: Airbus A330-203 (F-GZCP)

Operator: Air France

Fatalities: 228 (all onboard)

Cause: Pitot tube icing, autopilot disconnect, and pilot mismanagement leading to aerodynamic stall
