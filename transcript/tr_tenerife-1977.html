Date: March 27, 1977

Aircraft: KLM Boeing 747-206B (PH-BUF) & Pan Am Boeing 747-121 (N736PA)

Operator: KLM Royal Dutch Airlines & Pan American World Airways

Fatalities: 583 (all onboard KLM + most onboard Pan Am)

Cause: Miscommunication and premature takeoff in dense fog
