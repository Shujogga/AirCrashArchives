Pan Am Flight 103 – CVR Transcript

Date: December 21, 1988

Aircraft: Boeing 747-121 (N739PA)

Operator: Pan American World Airways

Fatalities: 270 (259 onboard + 11 on ground)

Cause: Terrorist bombing; explosive device hidden in luggage

[19:02:44] ATC: Clipper 103, you are cleared to the Papa Beacon, climb to and maintain flight level 90, right turn after takeoff proceed with heading 040
[19:03:02] PA: Ladies and gentlemen, we’ve lost pressurization. Please put on your oxygen masks.
[19:03:15] CAM-1: What the hell was that?
[19:03:27] CAM-2: Explosion felt aft.
[19:03:35] CAM: [sound of decompression]
[19:03:42] CAM-1: Mayday, mayday… we’re going down.
[19:03:50] CAM: [sound of impact]
[end of recording]
