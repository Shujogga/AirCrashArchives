Date: February 19, 2003
Aircraft: Ilyushin Il-76MD (15-2280)
Operator: Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC)
Fatalities: 275 (all onboard)
Cause: Controlled flight into terrain during descent in poor weather
No cockpit voice recorder transcript has been released publicly for this accident. The crash occurred during descent into Kerman Airport in deteriorating weather, including strong winds and low visibility. The aircraft struck the Sirch Mountains approximately 35 km southeast of the airport. All occupants were members of the IRGC, including several high-ranking officers. Investigators concluded the aircraft descended below minimum safe altitude in poor visibility, resulting in a controlled flight into terrain.
