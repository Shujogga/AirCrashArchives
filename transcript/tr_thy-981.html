Turkish Airlines Flight 981 – CVR Transcript

Date: March 3, 1974

Aircraft: McDonnell Douglas DC-10-10 (TC-JAV)

Operator: Turkish Airlines

Fatalities: 346 (all onboard)

Cause: Cargo door failure leading to explosive decompression and loss of control 

[12:30:00] CAM-1: Climb to FL230.
[12:30:05] CAM-2: Roger, FL230 set.
[12:30:10] CAM-3: Cabin pressurization normal.
[12:30:15] CAM: [sound of loud bang]
[12:30:16] CAM-1: What was that?!
[12:30:17] CAM-2: Rapid decompression!
[12:30:18] CAM-3: Cabin pressure dropping!
[12:30:20] CAM: [sound of rushing air]
[12:30:22] CAM-1: Controls not responding!
[12:30:24] CAM-2: We’ve lost hydraulics!
[12:30:26] CAM-3: Rear fuselage damage!
[12:30:28] CAM-1: Mayday, Mayday, Turkish 981, emergency descent!
[12:30:30] CAM: [sound of stick shaker]
[12:30:32] CAM-2: We’re in a dive!
[12:30:34] CAM-1: Pull up!
[12:30:36] CAM: [sound of impact]
[end of recording]
