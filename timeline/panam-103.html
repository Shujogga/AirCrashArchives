Pan Am Flight 103 – Detailed Timeline

🛫 Flight & Bombing – December 21, 1988
18:25 GMT – December 21, 1988

Pan Am Flight 103 departs London Heathrow en route to New York JFK aboard Boeing 747 “Clipper Maid of the Seas.”

18:58 GMT – December 21, 1988

Flight establishes radio contact with Shanwick Oceanic Control. All systems normal.

19:02:44 GMT – December 21, 1988

A bomb hidden in an unaccompanied suitcase explodes in the forward cargo hold. Aircraft disintegrates mid-air over Lockerbie, Scotland.

19:03–19:10 GMT – December 21, 1988

Wreckage falls across 845 square miles. 259 people aboard and 11 on the ground are killed instantly.

💥 Emergency Response & Investigation – December 21–28, 1988
Evening – December 21, 1988

Dumfries and Galloway Constabulary leads initial response. Local residents assist in search and recovery.

December 22, 1988

First meeting of investigators held. CIA and FBI join British authorities. Bombing suspected.

December 28, 1988

British investigators confirm explosion caused by a bomb in the luggage compartment.

⚖️ Legal Action & Legacy – 1989–2022
1991

Two Libyan intelligence agents indicted: Abdelbaset al-Megrahi and Lamin Khalifa Fhimah.

2001

Megrahi convicted of 270 counts of murder. Fhimah acquitted. Trial held in Netherlands under Scottish law.

2009

Megrahi released on compassionate grounds due to terminal cancer. Dies in 2012.

2022

Libyan national Abu Agila Masud taken into U.S. custody. Accused of building the bomb used in the attack.
