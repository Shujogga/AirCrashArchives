China Eastern Airlines Flight MU5735 was a domestic service from Kunming to Guangzhou. While cruising at FL291, the Boeing 737-800 entered a sudden near-vertical dive, briefly recovered, then plunged again into mountainous terrain. All 132 onboard were killed. The crash remains under investigation, with no final report released as of 2025.
