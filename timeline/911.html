September 11 Attacks – Detailed Timeline

🛫 Hijackings & Initial Impacts – September 11, 2001
07:59 EDT – September 11, 2001

American Airlines Flight 11 departs Boston for Los Angeles with five hijackers onboard.

08:14 EDT – September 11, 2001

United Airlines Flight 175 departs Boston with five hijackers onboard.

08:20 EDT – September 11, 2001

American Airlines Flight 77 departs Washington Dulles with five hijackers onboard.

08:42 EDT – September 11, 2001

United Airlines Flight 93 departs Newark with four hijackers onboard.

08:46 EDT – September 11, 2001

Flight 11 crashes into the North Tower of the World Trade Center between floors 93–99.

09:03 EDT – September 11, 2001

Flight 175 crashes into the South Tower of the World Trade Center between floors 77–85.

✈️ Pentagon Strike & Flight 93 Revolt – September 11, 2001
09:37 EDT – September 11, 2001

Flight 77 crashes into the Pentagon’s western façade, killing 125 on the ground.

09:57 EDT – September 11, 2001

Passengers aboard Flight 93 begin a revolt after learning of the other attacks.

10:03 EDT – September 11, 2001

Flight 93 crashes in a field near Shanksville, Pennsylvania, killing all 44 onboard.

💥 Tower Collapses & National Response – September 11, 2001
09:59 EDT – September 11, 2001

The South Tower collapses 56 minutes after impact.

10:28 EDT – September 11, 2001

The North Tower collapses 102 minutes after impact, and the Marriott Hotel is destroyed.

17:20 EDT – September 11, 2001

7 World Trade Center collapses due to fire damage.
