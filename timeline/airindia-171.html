Flight AI-171 was a scheduled international service from Ahmedabad to London Gatwick. Just 32 seconds after takeoff, the Boeing 787-8 Dreamliner lost thrust in both engines and crashed into a densely populated college campus. Of the 242 onboard, 241 perished, along with 19 fatalities on the ground. The sole survivor was seated near an emergency exit that detached during impact.

Both engine fuel control switches transitioned from RUN to CUTOFF within one second of each other, causing immediate loss of thrust. The Ram Air Turbine (RAT) deployed, and the crew attempted to restart both engines. Engine 1 showed signs of recovery, but Engine 2 failed to regain thrust. The aircraft was too low and slow to recover.
