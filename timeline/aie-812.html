Air India Express Flight 812 – Detailed Timeline

🛫 Departure & Approach – May 22, 2010
02:36 IST – May 22, 2010

Flight 812 departs Dubai for Mangalore aboard Boeing 737-800 VT-AXV with 166 people onboard.

05:32 IST – May 22, 2010

Aircraft contacts Mangalore ATC. Radar is unserviceable; procedural control is in effect.

05:47 IST – May 22, 2010

Cleared to descend from FL370. Crew begins ILS DME Arc approach to Runway 24.

⚠️ Unstabilised Approach – May 22, 2010
05:52–06:03 IST – May 22, 2010

Captain awakens from prolonged sleep. Aircraft is high on approach; gear deployed at 8,500 ft.

06:03 IST – May 22, 2010

First Officer issues three “Go Around” calls. EGPWS warnings of “Sink Rate” and “Pull Up” ignored.

06:05 IST – May 22, 2010

Aircraft touches down 5,200 ft down the 8,033 ft runway. Captain initiates late go-around after brake application.

💥 Overrun & Aftermath – May 22, 2010
06:06 IST – May 22, 2010

Aircraft overruns runway, strikes ILS antenna, breaches fence, and plunges into gorge. Fire erupts on impact.

Morning – May 22, 2010

Rescue teams recover 158 bodies. Eight survivors escape wreckage with injuries.

Following weeks

Investigation confirms captain’s failure to execute go-around as primary cause. Sleep inertia cited as contributing factor.
