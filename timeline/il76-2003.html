Iran Il-76 Crash – Detailed Timeline

🛫 Departure & Mission – February 19, 2003
Afternoon – February 19, 2003

Ilyushin Il-76MD (registration 15-2280) departs Zahedan Airport carrying 275 personnel of the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps on a classified mission to Kerman.

~17:24 LT – February 19, 2003

Aircraft enters descent phase toward Kerman Airport amid deteriorating weather conditions including strong winds and heavy snow.

⚠️ Loss of Contact & Impact – February 19, 2003
17:30 LT – February 19, 2003

ATC loses contact with the aircraft. Villagers near Sirch mountain report hearing a loud explosion.

~17:35 LT – February 19, 2003

Aircraft crashes into mountainous terrain ~35 km southeast of Kerman Airport. All 275 occupants perish.

💥 Recovery & Investigation – February 20–March 2003
February 20, 2003

Red Crescent and IRGC recovery teams reach crash site. Helicopters abort due to poor visibility.

February 21, 2003

Wreckage located ~100 meters below summit of Mt. Sirch. No survivors found.

March 2003

Investigation concludes crash was a controlled flight into terrain due to premature descent in poor visibility.
