Air India Express Flight 1344 – Detailed Timeline

🛫 Departure & Approach – August 7, 2020
14:14 GST – August 7, 2020

Flight 1344 departs Dubai for Kozhikode aboard Boeing 737-800 VT-AXH with 190 people onboard. It is a repatriation flight under the Vande Bharat Mission.

19:03 IST – August 7, 2020

Aircraft contacts Kozhikode ATC. Weather is poor with heavy rain and visibility around 2,000 meters.

19:21 IST – August 7, 2020

Windshield wiper on captain’s side fails. First landing attempt on Runway 28 is aborted due to poor visibility.

⚠️ Second Attempt & Unstabilized Approach – August 7, 2020
19:37 IST – August 7, 2020

Cleared to land on Runway 10. Aircraft approaches with tailwind and unstable descent profile.

19:38 IST – August 7, 2020

First Officer calls out unstable approach. Captain continues descent. Aircraft crosses threshold at 92 ft instead of 50 ft.

19:39 IST – August 7, 2020

Aircraft touches down 4,438 ft into the 8,858 ft runway. Thrust reversers deployed, then stowed, then redeployed, causing braking delay.

💥 Overrun & Aftermath – August 7–8, 2020
19:40 IST – August 7, 2020

Aircraft overruns runway, plunges 35 ft into a gorge. Fuselage splits into two. No post-crash fire reported.

Evening – August 7, 2020

Local villagers and CISF personnel initiate rescue. 21 fatalities confirmed, including both pilots. 110 injured.

August 8, 2020

AAIB begins investigation. Kozhikode Airport’s tabletop runway design and safety zone limitations come under scrutiny.

⚖️ Investigation & Findings – 2021–2022
September 2021

Final AAIB report cites pilot error, unstable approach, and failure to execute go-around as primary causes.

2022

Recommendations include improved CRM training, stricter SOP adherence, and enhanced runway safety infrastructure.
