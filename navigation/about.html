About This Archive

Our Mission

The Air Crash Timeline Archive is dedicated to documenting aviation accidents and incidents through detailed timelines, cockpit voice recorder transcripts, and investigative reports. We aim to preserve history, support research, and honour those affected by these events.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Chronological timelines of major and notable air accidents.
  • Transcripts and audio snippets from cockpit voice recorders.
  • Key facts: dates, locations, causes, and outcomes.
  • Searchable database—find incidents by year, airline, or location.

Why It Matters

By learning from past aviation accidents, the industry can improve safety protocols, design, and training. This archive serves as a centralized resource for enthusiasts, investigators, and professionals.

Get Involved

If you have additional data, CVR recordings, or corrections, please reach out to us at contact@aircrasharchive.com.
