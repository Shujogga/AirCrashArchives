Our Mission

The Air Crash Timeline Archive is a community-driven initiative dedicated to documenting the history of aviation incidents worldwide. Our goal is to provide a transparent, data-rich resource for enthusiasts, researchers, and educators who seek insight into the lessons learned from air disasters.

Meet the Team

Behind the archive is a dedicated group of aviation historians, engineers, developers, and crash investigators. We collaborate to gather flight data, analyze CVR transcripts, and build a timeline interface that's both informative and accessible.

How We Work

Our data comes from official reports, news archives, and public domain audio transcripts. We use modern web tools like CSV parsing and JavaScript timelines to organize and present the data clearly. As an open-source project, we welcome contributions, corrections, and feedback from users around the globe.
